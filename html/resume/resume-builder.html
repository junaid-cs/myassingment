A Complete Guide for Creating a Perfect Resume

Step 1: Include basic details

First things first, you need to carefully include the basic details of a perfect resume. These comprise contact details, residential address, email ID, marital status and the like.

Step 2: Sort out your employment history

Now, you need to decide what employment history needs to be included in the resume. It should highlight your employment journey, specific skill set, achievements and goals.

Step 3: Incorporate additional details

Apart from employment history, you should include additional details in your resume in the form of "Ad Hoc", "Additional Skills", "Creative Inclinations", and the likes.

Step 4: Try including transferable skills too

For instance, you can include or talk about interpersonal communication in the “General Skills” section. Some skills are transferable in nature and make a great impression of excellence on your employers when highlighted in the right manner.
