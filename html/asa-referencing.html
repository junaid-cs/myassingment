Does The ASA Style Need A Title Page?

Yes, the ASA style needs a title page. But, there are certain norms while creating the title page. You should add the title page separately with the complete title of the paper. The elements to be included are listed below:

 

  • Author names
  • Their institutions (listed vertically when there is more than one)
  • A running head of the shortened subject
  • An entire word count of the document
