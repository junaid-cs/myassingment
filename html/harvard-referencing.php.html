Where are Harvard Style References Used?

University students in the UK, USA (and other countries) commonly use the Harvard referencing style. All the sources directly cited feature in alphabetical order in the body instead of footnotes and are used in Social Science and Humanities. When using the Harvard referencing style, the Author and year appear as cited in texts, while complete source details features are in the reference list/bibliography at the end.

Of course, some universities publish their own formatting rules for the Harvard styles. Hence, always confirm any specific criteria or format that you must follow to ease your referencing list/bibliography and citation process.
