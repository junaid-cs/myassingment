How Should Students Write Footnotes In AGLC?

Here’s how students should use it while writing footnotes in AGLC referencing:

  • Author(s)
  • Judges
  • Title of the article
  • Year of publication
  • Volume and issue number of journal
  • Title of the journal
  • Starting page number of the article
  • Pinpoint references

 

 

Use these elements to write footnotes using the AGLC referencing style.
