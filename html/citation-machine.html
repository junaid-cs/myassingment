Most Popular Questions Searched By Students:

Q.1 How do you create a citation?

Your professor will give you a handbook of the citation style you need to follow. Follow the guidelines meticulously when you are citing your paper. Generally, in most citation styles, you need to add an in-text citation and reference page. However, in Chicago or oxford citation style, you need to use footnotes. You can also take advantage of various types of citation machine or citation tools available online, citing machine comes in free of cost.

Q.2 What should you include in a citation?

A citation will include detailed information about the source or author. These are the general information you are required to add:

  • Name of the resource
  • Name of the author
  • Name of journal (if applicable)
  • Published date
  • Accessed date

You might need to use other information varying on the kind of citation style you are using.

Q.3 What are the different types of citation styles?

Different citation styles exist in academic life. A particular citation style will be chosen as per your academic discipline. Here are a few citation styles you should know:

  • APA (American Psychological Association- Education, Psychology Sciences
  • MLA (Modern Language Association)- Humanities
  • Chicago – History, Business, Fine arts
  • Oxford- Legal authorities
  • Vancouver- Medicine, Science
  • Harvard- All academic disciplines

Q.4 How to use a citation machine?

Before using Citation Machine:
Choose from MLA, APA, Turabian, or Chicago as your citation style.
Select the type of items you want to mention, such as a book or a magazine article.
Fill in all the fields that Citation Machine requires (author, title, etc.), then select Make citation from the drop-down menu.

Q.5 What is the purpose of a citation machine?

A citation machine is essentially a works cited generator that searches the internet for relevant information and compiles it into a completely prepared bibliography that displays all the sources contributing to your work.

Q.6 How to cite a pdf on a citation machine?

Books, or excerpts from books, can be found in PDF format online. A copyright page with publication information is usually included in a book. To cite an online book, start with the standard information for a book and end with a URL or DOI.
Format: Last name, Initials. (Year). Book title. Publisher. DOI or URL
Reference entry: Sedgwick, E. K. (2003). Touching feeling: Affect, pedagogy, performativity. Duke University Press. https://www.bu.edu/honoringeve/files/2009/09/paranoid-reading-and-reparative-reading.pdf
In-text citation: (Sedgwick, 2003)

Q.7 How to insert YouTube video into citation machine?

To properly reference a YouTube video, you must consider the following details:
Full name, username, or business name of the person who created the content
The video’s title
The website’s title (YouTube)
The video's publisher
When was the video posted?
The video’s length or duration
The video’s URL
