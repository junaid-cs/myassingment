Tips for Writing a Thesis

You can contact anythesis helponline for expert tips. Such as,

  • Accept the limitation- The broader topic you will choose, the lengthier your paper would become. So, based on your knowledge and the required length of the thesis, limit your topic.

Structure of a Thesis

The structure of a written thesis is mainly divided into three parts. Such as,

Introductory part
  • Title page
  • Abstract
  • Preface 'Or' Acknowledgements (optional)
  • Table of contents
  • List of tables and figures
Main body
  • Introduction
  • Literature review
  • Data and methodology
  • Results
  • Discussion
  • Conclusion
  • Limitations and suggestions for future research
Finishing part
  • References
  • Appendices
