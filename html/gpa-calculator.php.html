    GPA Calculation of Weighted and Unweighted Cumulative GPA

    To find your semester's grade point average (GPA), use the formula below. Your credit score is divided by the number of credit hours.

    Semester GPA = total credit score /total credit hours

    Refer to the GPA Table

    A+ = 4.00 C+ = 2.33
    A - = 3.67 C = 2.00
    B+ = 3.33 D = 1.00
    B = 3.00 F = 0.00
    B - = 2.67  

    Suppose you got the GPA for each subject as

    English – 6.66; Mathematics – 6.00; Accounts – 12.00; & Economics – 7.4

    Use the calculation (credit points gained) / (grade points earned) to find your semester GPA.
